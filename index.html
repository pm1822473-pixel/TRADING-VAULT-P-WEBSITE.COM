1. Overview

What is TradeVault Pro?

TradeVault Pro is an all-in-one professional trading platform built for serious Forex and cryptocurrency traders. It combines 11 powerful modules into one clean, intuitive interface:

  • Smart Trade Journal — Log every trade with complete details
  • AI Chart Analyst — Instant ICT/SMC analysis with vision AI
  • Live TradingView Charts — Professional charting with indicators
  • Deep Analytics — Win rate, equity curve, setup performance
  • Calendar Heatmap — Visual P&L calendar by day
  • Session Timers — Kill Zone countdowns with notifications
  • Rules Checklist — Pre-trade discipline with ICT rules
  • 4 Calculators — Position size, R:R, profit target, drawdown
  • Fundamental Analysis — Economic calendar, news, sentiment
  • Train Your AI — Teach AI your trading style
  • Goals & Targets — Daily checklist, P&L targets, exports
✅ 100% Free to Start
No credit card needed. Full access to all core features. AI features require a free Groq API key (console.groq.com).
